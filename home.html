About Us

Astro Vastu is a blend of two disciplines: Astrology and Vastu Shastra. Vastu Shastra is an ancient Indian architectural science that focuses on the design and layout of buildings in harmony with natural forces, while astrology involves the study of celestial bodies and their influence on human life.

Building Structure Plans

Building structure plans are detailed architectural drawings that outline the design, dimensions, and specifications of a building. These plans typically include floor layouts, elevations, cross-sections, and structural details, serving as a blueprint for construction and ensuring the integrity and safety of the building.
